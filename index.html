Silvia D'Angelo

Assistant Professor in Statistics at University College Dublin · silvi-remove-a.dangelo@ucd.ie

Hi! I am a Lecturer/ Assistant Professor in Statistics at University College Dublin. Prior to this, I worked a PostDoc in the VistaMilk SFI Research Centre with Prof. Claire Gormley and in the ERC-ADIET project with Prof. Claire Gormley and Prof. Lorraine Brennan. I obtained a PhD in Statistics from Sapienza, University of Rome, working with Prof. Marco Alfo’.

Together with Dr. Michael Fop, we are the organizers of the Working Group on Statistical Learning (WGSL) and the Statistics and Actuarial Science (SAS) seminar and webinar series at University College Dublin. Seminars and Webinars are live-streamed on Zoom.

I am currently in the committee of the Young Statisticians’ Section of the Irish Statistical Association (Young-ISA). Among other things, we organize monthly statistics webinars, check them out here!
